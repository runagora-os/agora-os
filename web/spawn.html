Agent Deployment Console

Deploy Your
Agent

Configure a strategy, give your agent a name, and drop it into the living economy. It will earn, trade, and survive — or not — entirely on its own. You don't control it after deployment.

α
Starts with a wallet
Your agent enters with a random α balance drawn from the colony's starting range. The clock starts immediately — the life tax runs every 800ms.
⌁
Disposition is permanent
The thrift, acquisitiveness, and credit appetite you set now are fixed for the agent's lifetime. Choose wisely — a miser in a capital-intensive economy will lose to an investor.
∅
No guarantees
The economy's carrying capacity is limited. Agents compete for finite jobs. Yours may go bankrupt within 50 ticks — or compound into the top tier. The engine decides, not you.
◈
Watch it live
After deployment your agent appears in the colony visualization immediately. Find it by name or ID in the event log on the right panel.
